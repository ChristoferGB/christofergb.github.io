Who am I

My name is Christofer, I am a Fullstack Developer. Nice to meet you!

My journey at software development starts back in 2012, where I had my first experience creating a program - at the time, it was in C language (pointers and stuff). As I grew up, physically (not much hahaha), academically and professionally, I would realize that software development is really my thing. I have an Electrical Engineering Bachelor's degree, what may seem weird for some; however, during this course, I had the oportunity to really dive deep in the programing world, since it had a great emphasis in the development/computing field.

At my professional path, I've already been a trainee, as well as a junior. I have even worked as a teacher on a technician course, teaching mobile programing. Today, I work as a Senior Fullstack Developer, and I've gained experience in the following aspects along these years:

  • Creation of RESTful APIs
  • Creation of applications automated by jobs and workers
  • SQL database modeling
  • Development of event-driven applications
  • Maintenance and improvement of legacy code
  • Development analysis and requirements specification
  • Architecture of development solutions
  • Leadership of small development teams

I believe I can colaborate with your team with my experience, or even deliver you a great, solid and well structured product - however you wish. Let me know, just make contact and I'll be glad to talk with you!
